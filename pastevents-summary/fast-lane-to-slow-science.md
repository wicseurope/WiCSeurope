<h1>Fast Lane to Slow Science</h1>

<p>
  <strong>Event:</strong> Online panel discussion<br>
  <strong>Occasion:</strong> International Women’s Day<br>
  <strong>Date:</strong> 20 March 2023
</p>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>
This online panel discussion was organized to commemorate International Women’s Day and focused
on rethinking academic productivity, evaluation, and sustainability in scientific careers.
</p>

<p>
Panelists discussed the ideas introduced in the influential article <em>“Fast Lane to Slow Science”</em>
(<em>Trends in Cognitive Sciences</em>, 2019), exploring how current incentive structures affect research
quality, well-being, and equity in academia.
</p>
