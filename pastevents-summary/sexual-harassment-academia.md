<h1>Sexual Harassment in Academia: Confrontation and Coping Mechanisms</h1>

<p>
  <strong>Event:</strong> Keynote lecture<br>
  <strong>Conference:</strong> ESCOP 2019<br>
  <strong>Date:</strong> 25 September 2019<br>
  <strong>Location:</strong> Tenerife, Spain
</p>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>
This keynote lecture addressed sexual harassment in academic environments, focusing on
confrontation strategies and coping mechanisms.
</p>

<p>
The lecture took place as part of the conference <em>“Women's changing role in cognitive science in
the last 30 years”</em>, a tribute to Cristina Cacciari.
</p>
