<h1>Negotiating Challenges to Scientific Advancement</h1>

<p>
  <strong>Event:</strong> Panel discussion &amp; speed mentoring session<br>
  <strong>Conference:</strong> ICPS 2019<br>
  <strong>Date:</strong> 7 March 2019<br>
  <strong>Location:</strong> Paris, France
</p>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>
This panel addressed challenges such as disappointments, delays, and career setbacks in
scientific advancement. Speakers discussed strategies for resilience and navigating non-linear
career paths.
</p>
