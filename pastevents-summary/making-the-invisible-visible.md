<h1>Making the Invisible Visible</h1>

<p>
  <strong>Event:</strong> Panel discussion &amp; speed mentoring session<br>
  <strong>Context:</strong> Virtual SARMAC meeting
</p>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>
This event explored invisible forms of labor, bias, and inequality in academia. A panel
discussion was followed by a WiCS-Europe speed mentoring session aimed at supporting early-career
researchers.
</p>
