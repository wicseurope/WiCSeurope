<h1>Running Your New Lab: Tips and Tricks</h1>

<p>
  <strong>Event:</strong> Online panel discussion<br>
  <strong>Occasion:</strong> International Women’s Day<br>
  <strong>Date:</strong> 10 March 2022
</p>

<h3>Overview</h3>

<p>
This panel discussion focused on the challenges faced by early-career researchers when setting
up and leading a new research lab. Speakers shared practical advice on leadership, mentoring,
funding, and lab management.
</p>
