<p>
Rachelle Ho is a faculty member of the School of Psychology at the University of Aberdeen in Scotland. 
  Prior to this position, Rachelle completed her PhD at McMaster University, Canada where she used magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) 
  to evaluate brain networks in children with concussion or mild traumatic brain injury. 
  Following her PhD, Rachelle conducted further research to study depression in adults using MRI. 
  She is now the program coordinator of the Psychology of Mental Health program at the University of Aberdeen
  and studies the behavioural, emotional, and neurocognitive effects of mental health challenges, particularly in children.
</p>
